Community Salons


Company:
Community Salon is an advertisement company that promotes local business within the community. Local business are advertise through Community Salons web database, and digital frames located throughout local barber shops, style salons, beauty salons, day spas, and nail salons.
Purpose:
Community Salon is a company that gives back to local communities. We do this by partnering with the local salons/shops to display digital frames in their buildings. The revenue we generate from advertisement, we donate $5,000 to the local high school on behalf of all the salons who participate. Our business's goal is to pursue for a better tomorrow by teaming up with the local salons and giving back to the youth within the community.
